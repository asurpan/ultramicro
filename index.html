ULTRAMICRO PRO - OFFICIAL PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2024

Welcome to UltraMicro PRO. Your privacy is our absolute priority. This document outlines how we handle your information and why we require specific permissions to provide you with a professional audio experience.

1. Data Collection & Zero-Storage Policy

At UltraMicro PRO, we believe your conversations are your own. We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, audio, or location information. The app operates entirely on your device and does not have servers for storing user content.

2. Technical Permissions (Mandatory)

To deliver high-end audio routing during GSM calls, the app requires the following permissions:

  • RECORD_AUDIO (Microphone): Used strictly as a technical bridge to route audio to external headsets. WE DO NOT RECORD, SAVE, OR HEAR YOUR CALLS. This is an Android system requirement for any app that interacts with microphone hardware.
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: Required to detect when a phone call is incoming or active (OFFHOOK). This allows the app to activate protection automatically without user intervention.
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS: Allows the app to change the audio route from the internal microphone to your wired or Bluetooth headset during a call.
  • BLUETOOTH_CONNECT: Necessary on Android 12+ to manage and communicate with Bluetooth SCO headsets for professional audio quality.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to show a status icon, ensuring you know when the protection engine is active.

3. Advertising (AdMob)

The app integrates Google AdMob to support development through banner ads. AdMob may collect and use certain device identifiers (like the advertising ID) to serve personalized ads. You can review Google’s privacy practices at: Google Privacy & Terms.

4. Children’s Privacy

Since UltraMicro PRO does not collect any personal data, it is compliant with COPPA and safe for users of all ages.

5. Security Design

The app is built using modern Jetpack Compose and MVVM architecture, ensuring that audio routing is performed securely within the Android sandbox environment. We do not use third-party analytics trackers (like Firebase Analytics) to ensure maximum privacy.
